YOLOv8n — Object Detection

Nano model pre-trained on the COCO dataset (80 classes). Used for image and video detection.

Specifications

  • File: yolov8n.pt (~6 MB)
  • Architecture: YOLOv8 nano — optimized for speed
  • Training dataset: COCO (80 classes: person, car, dog…)
  • Output: bounding boxes + confidence score + class label
  • Threshold used: confidence ≥ 0.5

Image results

On input/test_image.jpg (parking lot scene):

  • 4 cars detected (class car)
  • Maximum confidence: 89.5%
  • Minimum confidence: 73.9%

Video results

On input/test_video_1.mp4 → output/test_video_2.mp4:

  • 721 frames processed at 1920×1080 @ 30 fps (~24 seconds)
  • Full annotated video generated by detect_video.py
  • Preview available on the results page

Related scripts: detect_image.py, detect_video.py, detect_tracking.py (base detection)

Speed vs. accuracy trade-off

YOLOv8n is the fastest model in the YOLOv8 family. Ideal for prototyping and CPU inference. For higher accuracy, yolov8s/m/l variants can be tested by changing MODEL in config.py.
